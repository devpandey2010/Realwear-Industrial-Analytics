**Insights From Pivot Table 2---Shift Analysis**

**Insight**                  :      **Findings**

Highest Productivity         :           Night shift

Longest Resolution Time     :         Night shift

Highest Command Failure Rate    :      Morning shift


**Night shift workers are most productive — possibly because**

Less distraction and noise at night

More experienced workers on night shift

Fewer concurrent sessions

**But night shift takes longest to resolve issues — possibly because:**

Less support staff available at night

Fewer expert teams online for remote assistance

**Morning shift has most command failures — possibly because**

Higher noise levels in the morning when factory is at full capacity

More workers using devices simultaneously causing network issues

-----

----**Recommendations**

**1 — Use devices only in low/medium noise areas**

Practical and immediately actionable 

No additional cost involved

But limitation — factory floors are inherently noisy, you can't always control where workers go 

**2 — Design device to focus on user's voice**

This is actually called Noise Cancellation Technology or Beamforming Microphone

Very innovative thinking 

But this is a hardware/R&D solution — long term and expensive 


**3.Network & Connection Optimization**

From our data connection type affects performance

Upgrade to stronger network in high noise plants

Better bandwidth = faster command processing = less failures

**4.Voice Command Training**

Train workers in high noise areas to speak commands differently

Slower, clearer pronunciation

Use simpler shorter commands

Low cost and immediately actionable 

**5.Shift Scheduling**

Schedule RealWear device usage during lower noise periods

Early morning or late night when factory noise is lower

Redistribute device usage away from peak production hours
