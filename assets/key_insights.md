**Insights From Pivot Table 2---Shift Analysis**

**Insight**                  :      **Findings**

Highest Productivity         :           Night shift

Longest Resolution Time     :         Night shift

Highest Command Failure Rate    :      Morning shift


**Night shift workers are most productive — possibly because**

Less distraction and noise at night

More experienced workers on night shift

Fewer concurrent sessions

**But night shift takes longest to resolve issues — possibly because:**

Less support staff available at night

Fewer expert teams online for remote assistance

**Morning shift has most command failures — possibly because**

Higher noise levels in the morning when factory is at full capacity

More workers using devices simultaneously causing network issues
